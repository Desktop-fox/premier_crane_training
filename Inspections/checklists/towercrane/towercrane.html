Tower Crane Inspection (Annual)

System/Part Item / Description Pass Fail N/A Remarks Photo
Base and Foundation
Foundation Inspect for cracks, settlement, or disintegration. Verify secure anchorage and ensure the base complies with manufacturer specs. (OSHA 1926.1402) & (ASME 5-2.1.1)
Levelness Ensure the base is level to prevent misalignment. (OSHA 1926.1402(a)) & (ASME 5-2.1.1)
Tower Structure
Mast Sections
Mast Sections Inspect for deformation, cracks, corrosion, or loose bolts. Verify proper alignment & secure connections. (OSHA 1926.1412(d)(1)(i)) & (ASME 5-2.1.3)
Climbing Frames
Climbing Frames Ensure proper functioning and structural integrity of climbing frames. Inspect locking mechanisms for wear or damage. (OSHA 1926.1412(c)) & (ASME 5-2.1.3(c))
Turntable and Slewing Ring
Turntable / Slewing Ring Check bolts & fasteners for tightness, wear. Inspect ring for cracks, proper lube, abnormal noise. (OSHA 1926.1412(d)(1)(ix)) & (ASME 5-2.1.3)
Boom and Jib (Luffing or Fixed)
Main Boom
Main Boom Inspect for cracks, bends, or structural damage. Verify indicators are operational. (OSHA 1926.1416(c)) & (ASME 5-2.4.2)
Jib
Jib (Fixed or Luffing) Inspect for deformation or damage. Check connection points for secure attachment. (OSHA 1926.1412(d)(1)(i)) & (ASME 5-2.1.9)
Guy Lines & Pendants Verify correct tension. Inspect for corrosion, wear, broken strands. (OSHA 1926.1413(a)) & (ASME 5-2.4.3)
Load Handling Equipment
Hooks & Latches
Hooks & Safety Latches Inspect hooks for deformation, cracks, proper safety latch operation. Verify free swivel. (OSHA 1926.1412(f)(2)(iii)) & (ASME 5-2.1.9)
Wire Ropes
Wire Ropes Inspect for frays, broken strands, kinks, corrosion, or flattening. Verify reeving per specs. (OSHA 1926.1413(a)) & (ASME 5-2.4.1)
Load Block & Sheaves
Load Block / Sheaves Check sheaves for alignment, wear, lubrication. Inspect load blocks for integrity & secure attachments. (OSHA 1926.1412(f)(2)(iii)) & (ASME 5-2.4.3)
Mechanical Systems
Hoist Mechanism
Hoist Mechanism Inspect drums, brakes, clutches for wear. Verify wire rope spooling. (OSHA 1926.1412(f)(2)(i)) & (ASME 5-2.4.2)
Counterweights
Counterweights Ensure secure installation, free of cracks/damage. Verify alignment & placement. (OSHA 1926.1412(f)(2)(iv)) & (ASME 5-2.1.6)
Safety Systems
Anti-Two-Block Devices
Anti-Two-Block Devices Ensure devices prevent overhoisting & alarms activate. (OSHA 1926.1416(d)) & (ASME 5-2.1.9)
Load Moment Indicators (LMI)
Load Moment Indicators Inspect calibration & operation. Verify displays & alarms. (OSHA 1926.1416(e)(1)) & (ASME 5-2.1.9)
Wind Speed Indicators
Wind Speed Indicators Confirm functionality, accuracy, and compliance with wind limits. (OSHA 1926.1431(k)) & (ASME 5-2.1.10)
Electrical Systems
Wiring and Connections
Wiring & Connections Inspect for cracked insulation, loose connections, corrosion. Verify grounding. (OSHA 1926.1412(d)(1)(xiv)) & (ASME 5-2.4.3)
Control Systems
Control Systems Check joysticks, pedals, E-stops for wear. Verify displays & indicators are functional. (OSHA 1926.1412(d)(1)(xiv)) & (ASME 5-2.1.8)
Environmental Considerations
Ground Conditions
Ground Conditions Verify level, stable ground for the crane and loads. Check for settling or changes. (OSHA 1926.1402) & (ASME 5-2.1.4)
Weather and Wind
Weather & Wind Ensure operations are within acceptable wind/weather. Inspect weatherproofing of components. (OSHA 1926.1431(k)) & (ASME 5-2.1.10)
Documentation
Inspection Reports
Inspection Reports Document results, deficiencies, corrective actions. Keep for 12 months. (OSHA 1926.1412(f)(7)) & (ASME 5-2.1.11)
Manufacturer Manuals
Manufacturer Manuals Verify manuals availability. Ensure inspections follow manufacturer specs. (OSHA 1926.1412(c)) & (ASME 5-2.1.11)
